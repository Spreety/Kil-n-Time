Welcome

Painting ceramics is the perfect getaway from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. It's relaxing and fun... and made even better when you can enjoy it with friends.

Children's ceramic painting classes are held each week on Thursdays from 1pm - 2:30pm. They will have a large variety of ceramics to choose from and have the opportunity to learn lots of fun painting techniques. terested in hosting a painting event and you just want to paint on your own, Call, text, send a Facebook message, or E-mail us to register.

The rest of my week is open for you to schedule and host your own ceramic painting party or event. Ceramic events can be held here or at the location of your choosing. I will bring all the ceramics and painting supplies to you. I will stay to help host the event, give an overview of the different kinds of paints we have, and teach a few of the techniques that you or your guest might want to try.
