How it Works

When you arrive, you will get to chose from the ceramics we have displayed. You will pay for your ceramic and the supplies/time fee, then we will sit down to paint. I will give you a quick overview of the different paints and techniques that we have available. I will answer questions and help you understand it better, if you need, while you are here.

Ceramics range in price from $.75 to $40.00
Supplies/time fee (covers the cost of all the supplies and my time): $5.00

If you would like, you can look through pictures of the ceramics that I have available and order a specific ceramic for the next time you come and paint. I need at least a week notice to have your ceramic ready.

If you select a large ceramic to paint and are not able to finish painting the ceramic in the time you are here, you are welcome to come back and finish painting your ceramic without having to pay the supplies/time fee again.

Ceramics To-Go

You can purchase a ceramic to-go If you would rather paint your ceramic at home. You will only be charged the price of your ceramic. No studio fee will be charged.

Children's Ceramic classes

Your children (ages 7 - 17) can join us every Thursday afternoon at 1pm-2:30pm to paint a ceramic and learn new techniques

Flat Rate Fee: $15.00 (covers ceramic plus all supplies) per class.

Host your own ceramic painting party or event

The rest of my week is open for you to schedule and host your own ceramic painting party or event. Ceramic events can be held here or at the location of your choosing. I will bring all the ceramics and painting supplies to you. I will stay to help host the event, give an overview of the different kinds of paints we have, and teach a few of the techniques that you or your guest might want to try.

The host of each painting event will receive a coupon for 20% off their next ceramic purchase.

This is a pricing chart for a group painting event. You add the reservation/supply fee to the ceramics fee to come up with the price for your event. I will bring as many ceramics as I have in the price range you select.

For a group of 5 to 8 people who want to paint ceramics in the $6 - $10 range, their party will cost $80. That covers everything. There will be no extra charges added. I will bring the ceramics and all the painting supplies. If requested, I will bring ceramics from the other price ranges. If a guest would like a more expensive ceramic, I will charge that guest the additional cost for the ceramic they chose.

For example: the party is for ceramics in the $6 - $10 range, but a guest wants to paint a ceramic that costs $16. I would charge that guest $6.00 to cover the cost of that ceramic.

These are the prices for a 2 hour painting event. Additional time will cost 1/4th the cost of your party for each half hour.

Call, text, send a Facebook message, or E-mail to schedule and set up your next event.
