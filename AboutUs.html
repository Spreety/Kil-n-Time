About Us

Hello! We are Roger and Jenny. My husband and I have been married for 16 years. We have three children. They are all teenagers now and are very helpful and supportive of this business. I (Jenny) am the owner of this new company. My family is my support system as we go through this business journey.

I love painting ceramics. I learned my love of ceramics when I was a child, painting ceramics with my family. Some of my favorite memories are from the times I spent sitting around the table with family and friends, painting ceramics, talking, and enjoying each other's company. Ceramics were a big thing back in the 80s. It was a really good time.

I want to bring back the ceramics that I knew as a child. I want this generation to experience ceramics the way that I was able to in my youth. I want to help others to make fond memories and grow their love of ceramics.

Kil'n Time Ceramics is a ceramic painting business. We sell greenware and ceramic bisque to be painted here or at your own home. We are a business that is run from our home. We have plans to expand our business with time and hope to one day have a building separate from our house. When customers come to our home, they will choose their ceramic then find a spot at the kitchen table to paint. There will always be yummy treats to enjoy and snack on while we talk and paint and enjoy each other's company. We encourage guests to host parties or group events. Painting is so much fun when you can enjoy it with your friends.
